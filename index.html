HARAJUKU

FINE JAPANESE CUISINE

Pune's finest Japanese dining experience with a live Teppanyaki counter - serving fresh Sushi, Sashimi and other Japanese delicacies.
Sit at the live table observing theatrical culinary drama or opt for the intimate crystal cocoon enclosed in lavish red leather and gold bamboos.

ADDAH

FINE INDIAN CUISINE

A beautiful rooftop lounge space, Addah, captures the fusion of the two elements Fire and Water. Time flies overlooking the expanse of the Pune skyline, the setting sun or the rising moon. Get lost in the rhythm of tranquil water of the pool on the O terrace as our chefs fire up the live charcoal counter to spice up your taste buds with Indian specialties.

SKY BAR

Located on the Rooftop, The Sky Bar offers a wide variety of spirits and cocktails. Enjoy your drink under the starlit sky or dip your feet in the reflecting pool seating - a perfect ambience for the evening.
